Our STRATEGIES


CREDIT

ASSESSING RISK WITH AN OWNER’S MENTALITY

Bridge invests in real estate-backed credit and industrial net-leased real estate occupied by high-quality credit tenants, targeting attractive current income with carefully-managed risk. We bring an “owner’s mentality” to our efforts and always seek to inspect what we expect.

Development & Opportunity Zones

BUILDING UPON WHAT WORKS

The Opportunity Zone legislation opened new doors for our team to bridge our knowledge of high-growth markets with tax benefits encouraging investment in America’s economically depressed communities.

LOGISTICS PROPERTIES

INNOVATIVE AND SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

The Bridge Logistics Properties team combines its operational and development DNA alongside deep customer, owner, and broker relationships to identify assets with pricing dislocation, an attractive basis, and opportunity for operational upside. Our team's capabilities and extensive footprint within our target markets seek to drive value for both our customers and investors.

NET LEASE

THE RIGHT PLACE AT THE RIGHT TIME

The Bridge Net Lease Team invests in mission critical industrial, manufacturing, logistics and other critical business infrastructure real estate located in Prime Growth and logistically connected markets leased to high-quality credit tenants.

OFFICE

ON THE FOREFRONT OF TARGET MARKETS

Bridge has a deep knowledge of professionally-oriented, resilient local markets where discounted office inventory is primed for investment. Our owner-operator reputation as a trusted partner attracts high-quality tenants and creates reliable value.
